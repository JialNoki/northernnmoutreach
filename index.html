Art as a transformative tool for healing and empowerment.

Welcome

Welcome to Northern New Mexico Outreach, where art becomes a bridge to healing, empowerment, and transformation. Through stilt walking, building, and creative workshops, we uplift at-risk youth, adults in halfway homes, and incarcerated communities.

Our Mission

We use art as a transformative tool to empower vulnerable communities. Through hands-on workshops in stilt building and walking, we foster resilience, self-confidence, and community through creativity, challenge, and movement.
